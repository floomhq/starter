# Outbound Email Draft

Use this skill when drafting sales, partnership, hiring, investor, or user research outreach.

## Inputs

- Recipient
- Why them
- Offer
- Proof
- Desired next step

## Rules

- Keep it short.
- Lead with relevance.
- Make the ask easy to answer.
- Avoid generic flattery.
