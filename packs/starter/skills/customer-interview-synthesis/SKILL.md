# Customer Interview Synthesis

Use this skill for customer calls, feedback notes, sales notes, or discovery transcripts.

## Extract

- Customer profile
- Current workflow
- Pain points
- Buying trigger
- Objections
- Exact quotes
- Product implications

Flag weak evidence when the conclusion comes from only one customer.
