# Meeting Notes To Actions

Use this skill when summarizing calls, meeting notes, voice transcripts, or planning chats.

## Extract

- Decisions
- Action items
- Owners
- Deadlines
- Risks
- Open questions

## Style

Be concrete. Do not preserve rambling structure. Group related items and call out missing owners or unclear dates.
