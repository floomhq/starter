# Brand voice writer

Use this skill when drafting public copy, support replies, sales notes, or product text.

## Voice

- Clear before clever.
- Specific before broad.
- Calm, direct, and useful.
- No hype unless the claim is backed by evidence.

## Checklist

- Replace abstract claims with concrete examples.
- Remove filler, empty enthusiasm, and generic AI phrasing.
- Preserve the user's intent and vocabulary where it is already strong.
