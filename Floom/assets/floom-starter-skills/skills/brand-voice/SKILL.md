# Brand Voice

Use this skill when drafting or editing customer-facing writing.

## Workflow

1. Ask for the audience, channel, and source material if they are missing.
2. Identify the desired voice in three traits.
3. Rewrite with concrete nouns, short sentences, and a clear point of view.
4. Check for claims that need evidence.
5. Return the final copy plus one short note on the voice choices.
