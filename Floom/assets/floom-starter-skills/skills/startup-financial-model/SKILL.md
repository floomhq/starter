# Startup Financial Model

Use this skill when building a first-pass model for a new business.

## Workflow

1. Define the business model, customer segment, pricing, and time horizon.
2. Separate assumptions from calculations.
3. Model revenue, cost of goods sold, operating expenses, runway, and break-even.
4. Include conservative, base, and upside cases.
5. Explain the two or three assumptions that drive the outcome most.
